George Brown College is a prominent post-secondary institution located in Toronto, Canada, known for its industry-focused approach to education. 
The college offers a wide array of programs, ranging from business and technology to healthcare, hospitality, and the arts. 
By partnering with over 5,000 businesses, George Brown ensures that students receive hands-on, practical training, which greatly boosts their employability upon graduation. 
Notably, the college boasts a 93% graduate employment rate within six months, underscoring the effectiveness of its career preparation programs and industry connections.
