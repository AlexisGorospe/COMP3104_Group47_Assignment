George Brown College is a leading post-secondary institution located in Toronto, Canada, recognized for its industry-driven educational approach. 
The college offers diverse programs across fields such as business, technology, healthcare, hospitality, and the arts, designed to meet evolving industry demands. 
With over 5,000 industry partnerships, George Brown equips students with real-world skills and practical experience, enhancing their job readiness upon graduation. 
Impressively, the college reports a 93% employment rate within six months of graduation, a testament to its strong industry connections and focus on career development.
