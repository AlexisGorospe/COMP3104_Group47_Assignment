### What is DevOps?

- **DevOps** integrates **development, IT operations, and security** through:
  - Automated workflows
  - Optimized collaboration across teams

### Key Benefits:
- Faster, more reliable software delivery
- Continuous improvement and innovation
- Resilience in adapting to changing market conditions

### CI/CD and Security:
- **Continuous Integration/Delivery (CI/CD)** ensures real-time feedback and rapid iteration.
- **DevSecOps** embeds security early in development to mitigate risks and improve system integrity.

### Fun Fact:
- Top-performing organizations using DevOps deploy updates **46 times faster** than traditional models, achieving both **agility** and **stability**.
