DevOps integrates development and IT operations by automating and streamlining workflows.
It enables faster, more reliable software delivery and seamless team collaboration.
DevOps emphasizes continuous integration and continuous delivery (CI/CD), ensuring rapid feedback and quicker response times.
Security is also a key focus in DevOps, with practices like DevSecOps integrating security from the start of the development process.
A key benefit is its ability to reduce lead time for changes, with top-performing organizations deploying updates 46 times faster than their peers.
