George Brown College is a public, fully accredited college of applied arts and technology in Toronto, Ontario. It was founded in 1967 and offers a wide range of programs, including those in arts, business, community services, and engineering technology.

George Brown College provides both full-time and part-time programs across three campuses in downtown Toronto. It is known for its hands-on approach to education.

George Brown also offers industry partnerships and placements that give students real-world experience before they graduate.

George Brown College provides various industry certifications to prepare students for their careers.
