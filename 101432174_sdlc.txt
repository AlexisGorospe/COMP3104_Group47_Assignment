### What is SDLC?

The **Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC)** is a **comprehensive framework** guiding software development teams through:

- **Planning**
- **Design**
- **Development**
- **Testing**
- **Deployment**

### Benefits of SDLC:
- Ensures timely delivery and adherence to budgets
- Meets **quality standards** throughout the project
- Reduces project failure rates by up to **40%**

### Fun Fact:
- **Agile frameworks** integrated with SDLC boost customer satisfaction by enabling **continuous feedback** and **iterative improvements**, making projects more adaptable.
